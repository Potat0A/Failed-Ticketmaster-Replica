About Ticketmaster

Ticketmaster is a global leader in live event ticketing, connecting millions of fans with their favorite artists, teams, and experiences worldwide. For over 40 years, we've been dedicated to providing seamless and secure access to the best in music, sports, and entertainment.

Our Mission: Unforgettable Experiences

Our mission is simple: to make every event unforgettable. We strive to create a positive and efficient experience for both fans and event organizers, from the moment tickets are purchased to the final curtain call (or buzzer!).

Connecting Fans and Events

We leverage cutting-edge technology and a vast network to ensure fans can easily find and purchase tickets to the events they love. Our platform offers a wide range of features, including:

  • Secure ticket purchasing and delivery options
  • Personalized recommendations and event discovery tools
  • Mobile ticketing for easy access on event day
  • Customer support available 24/7 via phone, email, and chat

Our Commitment

We are committed to:

  • Providing a safe and secure ticketing experience, combating fraud and protecting fan data.
  • Supporting the artists, teams, and venues that bring live events to life.
  • Continuously innovating to improve the fan experience and enhance the ticketing process.
  • Supporting local communities through various initiatives.
